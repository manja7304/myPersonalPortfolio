Choosing a Vector Database for Production RAG

Manjunath K GMar 20268 min read

Pinecone vs Chroma vs pgvector — trade-offs for security AI workloads.

1. pgvector

Single DB for app data + vectors; good for moderate scale.

2. Pinecone

Managed, low ops overhead, strong for high-throughput retrieval.

3. Chroma

Fast prototyping, embeddable, good for dev and small deployments.
